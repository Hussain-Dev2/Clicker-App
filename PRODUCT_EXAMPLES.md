## Price Point Suggestions

### Budget Options (5,000 - 10,000 points)
- Small game currency packs
- 1 week subscriptions
- Low-value gift cards ($5)

### Mid-Range (10,000 - 25,000 points)
- $10-$15 gift cards
- Monthly subscriptions
- Medium game currency packs

### Premium (25,000 - 50,000+ points)
- $25-$50 gift cards
- 3-month subscriptions
- Large game currency packs

## Tips for Product Management

1. **Keep It Organized**
   - Use consistent naming: "[Platform] [Value]"
   - Group similar products by category
   - Update stock regularly

2. **Set Realistic Prices**
   - Consider how long it takes users to earn points
   - Match real-world value (e.g., $1 = 1000 points)
   - Offer varied price points

3. **Stock Management**
   - Use unlimited for codes you can regenerate
   - Set limits for actual purchased cards
   - Monitor popular items

4. **Image Guidelines**
   - Use official logos when possible
   - Keep aspect ratio consistent
   - Ensure images load quickly

5. **Descriptions**
   - Clear and concise
   - Mention what users can do with it
   - Add platform/game name
